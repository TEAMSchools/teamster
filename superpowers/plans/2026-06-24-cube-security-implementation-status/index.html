    Key decisions / gotchas (now also in CLAUDE.md)

    • Built from marts (intra-mart refs), not int_people__staff_roster — marts/CLAUDE.md forbids ref-into-marts.
    • Layer-2 detail gate is OR (below-rank OR downline), not AND — leadership sees everyone below in scope; same-level peers hidden unless they report in; same-level reports (Dean→Teacher) stay visible. See spec § Access model.
    • WITH RECURSIVE → contract: enforced: false (contract wraps SQL in a subquery). --favor-state beats stale-dev-shadow. dim_staff is all-time, not active-only. entity from business_unit_name. (All in src/dbt/CLAUDE.md / src/dbt/kipptaf/CLAUDE.md.)
