Survey Windows for all regions: April 7 - April 25, 2025

Surveys this Round:

  • Support Survey: Your feedback will provide direction on how school operations teams and KTAF teams (HR Ops, Data, Technology) can improve this year and how they can incorporate your feedback in their planning for next year.
  • Manager Survey: This is where you provide feedback about your manager. It is prefilled based on the person you report to in ADP.
  • Gallup Q12 Survey: This survey is administered by Gallup. Check your email for your unique Gallup link (it won’t be on Survey HQ).
  • Insight TNTP Survey: This survey is administered on behalf of the KIPP Foundation. Check your email for your unique TNTPlink (it won’t be on Survey HQ).
  • Staff Info & Cert Update: This survey is always available for you to update demographic and certification information. If you’ve completed it in the past year, you do not have to complete it again.

How to access your surveys:

  1. Google Chrome is required to access all surveys. Please follow this help guide if you are not using Chrome.
  2. Sign into Survey HQ with the same username and password that you are using for Okta.
  3. Find the survey link for the survey you are assigned and click to open the survey.
  4. You must be signed into your KIPP Google account to access all surveys. Sign into Google with:
    • username@apps.teamschools.org if you are a New Jersey teammate or a member of KTAF
    • username@kippmiami.org if you are a Miami teammate.
